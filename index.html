Our Vision

The small seed of thought was based on the years of action that the originator of this movement Jacob Vadakanchery had starting from his native village of Vypeen which was based on Gandhiji’s concept of Svaraj and self reliant life. The Father of the Nation envisioned a life based on the essential confidence and pride of being independent and in control of one’s own body and mind. To be in tune with the essential natural balance of the human body he evolved a special system of Nature Cure that is also our leading light. As Gandhiji said “ Nature Cure is a way of life, not a course of treatment” .The Nature Curer does not “ sell a cure” to a patient. Instead he leads the person to a right way of in his home which will not only cure him of a particular ailment but also save him from falling ill again. We believe that the best healer is the person who is afflicted and experiencing the discomforts of a body struggling to correct itself. The healer should prepare the space and provide the time to listen with patience by giving a helping hand using the most simple and natural means for correction to take place. For healing is love made visible.
